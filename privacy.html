Last Updated: October 7, 2025

SpotAlert Inc. (“SpotAlert”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform, services, or apps (“Services”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email, password, and any optional profile details.

2. How We Use Information

3. Data Security

We use encryption and AWS infrastructure to protect your stored and transmitted data. No method of storage is 100% secure; however, we apply commercially acceptable means to safeguard it.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and alert data only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes. Users can delete their accounts anytime, which permanently removes data unless required by law.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent user data. We may share limited data with trusted partners solely for alert delivery, email notifications, and payment processing (e.g., Stripe, AWS SES).

Your data may be processed and stored in different regions where our infrastructure operates. By using our platform, you consent to cross-border data transfer under these protections.
